# 1.Marketing Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive **Power BI** project designed to track, analyze, and visualize marketing performance across various products, regions, and channels. This dashboard provides actionable insights into ROI, spending efficiency, and revenue trends from January 2023 to November 2024.

## 2.Project Overview

The goal of this project was to create a centralized reporting tool for marketing stakeholders to monitor campaign health. It translates complex marketing data into easy-to-digest KPIs and interactive visuals, enabling data-driven decisions regarding budget allocation and regional strategy.

## 3.Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Avg ROI:** 4.48
* **Avg CPC:** 6.57
* **Avg CTR:** 13.86%
* **Total Revenue:** $1.04M
* **Total Spend:** $261.17K
* **Total Clicks:** 56K
* **Impressions:** 570K

## 4.Key Features & Visualizations

* **Revenue vs. Spend Analysis:** Comparative bar charts for Marketing Channels (Email, Social Media, etc.) and Regions to identify the most profitable segments.
* **Product Performance:** A donut chart showing revenue distribution, highlighting **Product C** as the top contributor (33.1%).
* **Regional Matrix:** A detailed breakdown of sales across East, North, South, and West regions for Products A through D.
* **Quarterly Trends:** An area chart visualizing revenue fluctuations across Q1–Q4 to identify seasonal patterns.
* **Spending Flow:** A waterfall chart depicting the increase/decrease in spend across different quarters and channels.
* **Dynamic Filtering:** Date slicer functionality to drill down into specific timeframes.

## 5.Tech Stack

* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Modeling:** Power Query (M) for ETL processes.
* **Calculations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom measures like ROI, CTR, and CPC.
* **Data Source:** (e.g., Excel/CSV Marketing Datasets)

## 6.Insights Derived

1. **Top Product:** Product C is the primary revenue driver, accounting for roughly **$0.35M (33.1%)** of total revenue.
2. **Channel Efficiency:** "Email" and "Social Media" show a high revenue-to-spend ratio compared to other channels.
3. **Regional Strength:** The **East** region consistently performs well across all product categories.
4. **Trend Analysis:** Revenue shows a steady climb through Q1 and Q2, with stabilization in the later half of the year.
